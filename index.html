Week #1 -> Short Bio

This activity of presenting ourselves reminds me of my early days in elementary school. the teacher would always ask us to give some information about ourselves in some sort of activity. Sometimes, we would get handed out questionnaires with little boxes that needed to be filled out, make drawings our houses, family, friends, or of us. But, my favorite one was making acronyms with my name. I would write down my name vertically and write a word that is representative of me at each letter. I have done these dozens of times, but I wanted to do it again for this bio, but with a twist. So, I have asked ChatGPT to create the acronym with words that he thinks represent me, both negative and positive, based on our previous conversations. Let's see what came out and if it is True:

  • Adventurous: I am adventurous, I love hiking, biking and travel, always down for an adventure.
  • Resourceful: I am resourceful as well, I usually don't like asking for help unless I am really stuck, I think it is really beneficial to find what you need to know yourself.
  • Nuturing: I think this is also true, but not as much as the last two. I am not the most nuturing friend, this is something I could work on.
  • Analytical: I think this is also accurate. I really like analyzing results, looking deep into subjects, and I am good at discerning certain things from situations.
  • Understanding: Very accurate as well. I think I am very understanding and tend to not see things that happen to me as personal. I am rarely frustrated when friends cancel on me and also understanding in profesional environments.
  • Determined: Not as much as I would like. There are a lot of things I am not willing to sacrifice to accomplish my goals such as time off, comfort, alone time and many others. I still work hard but not as much as other people.
  • ----
  • Joyful: That is very much true. I am often being called a "golden retriever" since I'm always making jokes and trying to lighten the mood.
  • Adaptable: Yes, I feel like this is similar to "understanding". I can easily adapt to different environment and situations. I will not necessarily fit right in everywhere but will always be able to adapt myself to get along.
  • Benevolent: That is true as well. I really like taking some time to help out other people, but I do have my limits as well. I am very self-reliant, so I do expect people to be too. I don't mind giving some time to help out, but I don't like when people dump their responsibilities on me.
  • Loyal: This is something that is very important to me. Respecting engagements and being loyal to your words is something that I always do and that I admire in other people. Trust in your friends, partners and colleagues is something that is hard to achieve but when done right gives you the opportunity accomplish a lot.
  • Energetic: Also true. This goes back to my golden retriever attitude. I always have energy and always willing to do something. But, I feel like I have lost some of my drive and the last 2 years. Maybe I'm getting older, or my priorities are changing, I don't know...
  • Respectful: This is also a priority for me. Respecting yourself and others is the key to establishing healthy relationships with others.
  • Trusthworthy: Very similar to loyalty, it is important that my act on what I say. I expect the same from others, so I act the way I wish other would act with me.

All in all, I feel like ChatGPT did a good job(which does not happen often). He did forget to put the "b" in "Jalbert" three times even when I told him. The answers he gave me are also pretty generic, but it did give me a playground to talk about myself.
